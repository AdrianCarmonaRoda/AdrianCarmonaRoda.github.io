CrimeBoss: Rockay City

For two years, I served as a Level Scripter and Designer on CrimeBoss: Rockay City, responsible for maintaining optimal game balance and ensuring smooth, engaging gameplay flow throughout the experience. This involved iterative design, scripting, and playtesting to achieve desired player experiences.

During my time there I worked more specificaly in the Character Missions, special missions that player could play to see more of each character story and lore.

Wiz 01

As all of Wiz missions, wiz 01 is a puzzle mission more than a classic shooter one, for so I needed to implement and prototype o lot of custom logic for the level, like the cart system below the player, the counter for chess pieces, all the necessary VO's for each situation...

Wiz 02

This level challenged players to locate a concealed floor drain and discreetly deposit a specially prepared bar of soap containing a hidden message, avoiding detection from patrolling guards throughout the process.

To support mission replayability, I prototyped a system for dynamically selecting floor vent locations each playthrough. This required designing and scripting guard patrol paths to ensure consistently navigable challenges, alongside implementing all voice-over dialogue to provide players with contextual understanding beyond on-screen information, enhancing overall level clarity and immersion.

Wiz 04

This level challenged players to escape a small apartment by destroying evidence – erasing hard drives and burning documents – before exiting through a window. The subsequent escape route involved climbing to the rooftops, navigating a fence under enemy fire, and finally crossing to another building via an electrical cable.

This level presented significant technical challenges requiring extensive prototyping and custom functionality implementation. The existing climbing system limited interaction to ladders; therefore, I had to work around a solution enabling full surface climbability for the fence sequence.

Additionally, the final cable traversal segment required bespoke scripting to adhere the player to the cable while restricting movement to forward and backward directions. This included implementing scripted fall events at specific locations, demanding precise timing and player control to avoid failure.

Wiz 05

This level represented the most action-oriented segment within the Wiz pack, challenging players to defend both themselves and a refueling aircraft as they prepare for escape from the country. Players must manage multiple dynamic events while contending with continuous waves of enemy combatants.

The design focused on intense shooter gameplay, requiring players to actively protect themselves and key objectives under sustained pressure – emphasizing reactive engagement and strategic prioritization amidst escalating threats.

Significant effort was dedicated to designing dynamic enemy wave patterns, including spawn locations and timing, requiring extensive playtesting to achieve balanced challenge. Beyond combat, the level incorporated interactive elements such as refueling the aircraft, moving cart out of the way, and successfully boarding the plane during takeoff.

Jupiter 02

This fast-paced level emphasizes player reaction time and strategic thinking under pressure. It begins with a dialogue exchange between Jupiter and members of the mafia, quickly escalating into an intense gunfight.

Players are presented with limited time to assess the situation and formulate an escape strategy, demanding quick reflexes and decisive action throughout the encounter.

Parts of the level needed to be redesigned and play tested carefully to ensure adequate space and clear pathways supported the fast-paced decision-making required by the gameplay.

Furthermore, careful consideration was given to the camera direction during the introductory cinematic to provide players with a comprehensive overview of the environment and situation before engaging in action.
